About The Fest

Since the inception of Bosco Fest in 1984, Don Bosco School, Park Circus, proved to be a haven of talent and opportunity. The pioneer of inter-school festivals in Kolkata, Bosco Fest had its humble beginnings in only two events: Bosco Beat, the Western Band competition, and Bosco Raag, the Classical Music event. Gradually, as the years passed, like a Rolling Stone, it gathered momentum with new and exciting additions of marquee events in its arsenal, like Bosco Tango, the Western Dance extravaganza and Bosco Impact, a stage for aspiring thespians. Now, in its 35th incarnation, dare to venture into the excitement of the undiscovered.

Slip inside the eye of your mind. A new world awaits. A new world where titans of the times of yore will live and breathe again. As we progress, we must take a step back and look at the world as it was when a revolution of peace and new beginnings ran rampant, ushering in an era where lives were forever changed. A new millennium was preceded by a century of innovations and inspirations. Summon the courage and boldly go where no man has gone before.

Hit the ‘Rewind’ button and run to the hills to put things back into perspective as the world is transformed before your very eyes. ‘Relive’ the wonder years, where time stood still and conventional culture was countered on every step of the way; and where the line between myths and legends was blurred. Orchestrate an ode to these storied heroes and heroines by taking to a stage where we have always strived to push the boundaries of talent.

Electrify an entire generation like Jimi Hendrix in Bosco Beat.

Be the Ravi Shankar to a George Harrison in Bosco Jukebox.

Let no one put you in a corner as you steal the limelight and stop hearts in Bosco Tango. Enchant your audience with the nuances of classical music in Bosco Raag.

Reimagine the future as we take you back to where it all began.
